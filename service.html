Community Service

Giving back to my community through volunteering and teaching has played a huge role in shaping my character and values.

I have participated in organizations such as Key Club, that have allowed me to give back to my community by volunteering at events and places such as the Woodlands Marathon , the Houston Food Bank, and Texas Children's Hospital.

I have also worked for the Texas A&M University College of Engineering as a student assistant. Because I had great mentors while starting my coding journey, I also wanted to fulfill that mentor role for other students who were starting to dive into the coding world. As a student assistant, I was able to help teach freshman engineering students fundamental programming concepts and coding in Python.

Throughout my experiences, I have seen the positive impact that serving others can have and because of this, it has become one of the main reasons why I want to continue my career as a software engineer. Being able to create technology that can positively impact the lives of many is something that I will always strive for.
